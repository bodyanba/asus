    Blockchain Terminal

    A professional trading tool for everyone

    Watch the video
    The Blockchain Terminal is a Linux-based system that offers a fully customizable trading platform. Built for any level of trading expertise, from day traders to institutional investors, users can select from Basic or Advanced Modes depending on their experience level or trading needs.

    The trading platform that removes barriers

    The BCT Thin Client Hub features an ultra-compact design, but it is what's inside that counts. The BCT Hub is designed to make a big impact, both in terms of performance and capabilities.

    User flexibility

    Traders have instant access to a variety of different apps and tools to augment their trading experience. Whether initiating a trade, viewing different portfolio chart ranges, checking history, sending or receiving coins, or searching for trends on social media, the Blockchain Terminal allows users to tailor the interface according to their requirements.

    Seamless Integrated Design

    The elegantly-designed Blockchain Terminal features smooth lines and an understated finish. The integrated BCT Thin Client Hub seamlessly connects to the BCT Monitor. And with a thickness of just 16mm, it frees up precious desktop space.

    Full Speed Ahead

    Speed can mean all the difference when it comes to maximizing trading profits. For those who are serious about leveraging every edge they can get, the Blockchain Terminal utilizes a smart order routing system that takes advantage of the most minute price differences among the various exchanges during trade execution. This translates into maximized returns on every transaction and increased profits.

    The BCT Terminal interfaces with 160+ exchanges, trading all major cryptocurrencies. With secure low-latency access, and a consolidated order book, trades execute at high speeds. All exchanges are accessed by a unified KYC registration.

    Real-time ComplianceGuard (CG) captures all trading logs on a private fork of the Ethereum network. This simplifies proactive internal audits and fund governance.

    Powered by Linux, the platform boasts reliability, uptime, and hardened security. Only certified devices are allowed to be connected in the closed ecosystem. The platform’s software prioritizes security, efficiency, and simple DevOps community integration.

    The customizable screen displays applications, including market data, chats, news, ICO cashflow, spreadsheets, calendars, and all data recorded in the CG Blockchain logbook.

    The Order Management System enables you to trade across multiple crypto exchanges from a single wallet, providing the best available prices.

    The BCT Token powers all App subscriptions as an ERC20 token and is easily stored. Our open App community is offered a revenue share with all approved Apps, encouraging an open development environment.
